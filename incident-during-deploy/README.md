# The Coincidence

Mid-deploy. Stage 23 of 47. An unrelated service goes down.

Deploy running. Incident happening. Two things at once.

*The United Airlines outage of July 2024: a hardware failure in their network gear cascaded into a system-wide ground stop. 3,500 flights delayed. The FAA initially thought it was a cyberattack. It was a single network component. United's post-mortem: "We had a single point of failure we didn't know about." Every production incident is a test of your unknown unknowns.*
